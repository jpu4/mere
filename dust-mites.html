Dust Mites

Dust mites are small arthropods that live in your home and feed on flaked-off human skin. Although they are microscopic, they can cause serious health issues. Even if you’re a neat freak, if there’s dust, there’s a strong possibility you have dust mites.

Dust mites can cause allergies, sneezing, coughing, wheezing, skin irritations, and even eye trouble such as redness and watering. More serious symptoms are Eczema and also an increase in risk for asthmatics.
